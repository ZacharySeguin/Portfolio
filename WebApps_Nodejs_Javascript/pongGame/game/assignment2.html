Click either one of the buttons below to select your player.

Game Information: This is a simple multiplayer pong game, it is meant to be played by two players, but multiple spectators
can watch the game. After any players score, the game will freeze and wait for both players to click on the screen again before
starting a new round. After a player reaches a score of 5, that player will be crowned as the winner, and the game will reset to
it's original player selection phase to be started again. Each time the ball hits a paddle, it increases in speed!
